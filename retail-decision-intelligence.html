Project Overview

A retail analytics project developed for Naan Mudhalvan TalentHack 2K26 by HyperLaunch, using more than one million transaction records to identify customer behaviour, revenue trends and business opportunities.

Problem Addressed

Retail businesses need clear customer segments, seasonality insights and product-level performance data to make better marketing and inventory decisions.

My Contribution

Performed data cleaning, exploratory analysis, customer feature engineering, clustering and dashboard development.

PROJECT WORKFLOW

How the system works

1

Load Dataset

Import the Online Retail II dataset.

2

Clean Data

Remove missing customer IDs, cancellations, duplicates and invalid values.

3

Create Revenue

Calculate revenue from quantity and price.

4

Explore Trends

Analyse monthly revenue, products, countries and customers.

5

Build RFM

Create Recency, Frequency and Monetary features.

6

Scale Features

Prepare customer features for clustering.

7

K-Means

Segment customers into meaningful groups.

8

Validate Clusters

Review cluster quality and business meaning.

9

Power BI

Build KPIs, filters and interactive visuals.

10

Recommend Actions

Turn analytical insights into business decisions.
